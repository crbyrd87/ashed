Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ashed ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Chris Byrd, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Ashed application ("App").

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the app. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: username, email address, display name, date of birth (for age verification)
  • Profile information: profile photo, bio, location (optional)
  • Cigar check-ins: cigar name, brand, vitola, rating, tasting notes, photos
  • Social activity: friend connections, comments, reactions ("fires")
  • Humidor and wishlist contents
  • Feedback and bug reports you submit through the App

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data: features used, screens visited, actions taken (via PostHog analytics)
  • Device information: device type, operating system, browser type
  • Location data: approximate location when you search for nearby venues (only when you use the venue feature)
  • Log data: IP address, access times, errors

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Providing and operating the App Account info, check-ins, humidor, social data
Personalizing your experience Check-in history, ratings, preferences
AI-powered recommendations and pairings Check-in history, ratings, flavor tags
Finding nearby cigar venues Location (only when you use venue search)
Sending transactional emails Email address
Improving the App Anonymized usage analytics via PostHog
Responding to support requests Email, feedback content
Preventing fraud and abuse Account info, usage data, IP address

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Your data is stored securely on Supabase's servers. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — AI-powered features including cigar recommendations, tasting notes, and the band scanner. Check-in data may be sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API — Venue search and location data. Privacy Policy
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (password resets, notifications). Privacy Policy
  • PostHog — Anonymous usage analytics to help us understand how the App is used. No personally identifiable information is shared. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — App hosting and delivery. Privacy Policy

We do not sell your personal information to any third party, ever.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent — when you choose to make your profile or check-ins public
  • Service providers — the third-party services listed above, only as needed to operate the App
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business transfer — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to users

5. Public and Private Content

By default, your check-ins are public and visible to other Ashed users. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy:

  • Private profile — your profile and check-ins are only visible to friends
  • Private check-ins by default — all new check-ins are private unless you choose to share them
  • Individual check-in visibility can be set when logging each smoke

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through Settings, your personal information and check-in data are permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update your information through Settings → Account
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data through Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Export — export your check-in journal through Settings → Account → Export My Journal
  • Opt out of analytics — contact us to opt out of PostHog analytics tracking

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at chris@ashed.app.

8. Children's Privacy

Ashed is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will promptly delete their information and terminate their account.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Row-level security on all database tables
  • All API keys stored server-side, never exposed in the app
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Passwords hashed and managed by Supabase Auth

While we take security seriously, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

10. Cookies and Tracking

The App uses minimal cookies and local storage to maintain your session and preferences. We use PostHog for analytics, which may set cookies to track usage patterns. You can opt out of analytics tracking by contacting us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
